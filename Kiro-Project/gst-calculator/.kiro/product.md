# Product Overview

A simple, elegant GST (Goods and Services Tax) Calculator for Indian tax rates.

## Purpose
Allows users to quickly calculate GST amounts and final prices by entering a base amount and selecting from standard Indian GST rates (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%).

## Key Features
- Base price input with validation
- Four standard GST rate options
- Real-time calculation of GST amount and total
- Clean, accessible UI with responsive design
- Educational information about GST rate categories

## Target Users
Indian businesses, shopkeepers, and consumers who need quick GST calculations.
